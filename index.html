The {{ site.title }} is a resource and forum for phase field practitioners ranging from novices to seasoned code developers. The contents of the {{ site.title }} is developed through community involvement with a special emphasis on providing links and information about codes and benchmark problems for phase field modeling. The primary goals are to host resources that

  • provide a set of benchmark problems that facilitate comparing and contrasting phase field codes as well as provide pedagogical examples for teaching and learning phase field methods,
  • increase the adoption of phase field methods in engineering and scientific applications and aid in the development of quantitatively predictive phase field modeling,
  • and, that foster an engaged and integrated phase field community with special emphasis on providing networking opportunities for graduate students and post-docs.
